Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Meta Advertising Program?

The Meta Advertising Program is an exclusive opportunity for content creators to earn $3000 per week by promoting major brands on their social media platforms. We provide all training and materials needed to succeed.

How much does it cost to join?

There is absolutely no cost to join our advertising program. This is a legitimate paid opportunity, not a paid program. We invest in our advertisers because we believe in the value they bring to our brand partners.

What are the eligibility requirements?

You must be at least 18 years old, have an active social media presence (any platform), and be able to commit 5-10 hours per week to the program. No previous advertising experience is required.

How and when will I get paid?

Payments of $3000 are processed every Friday directly to your bank account or PayPal. The first payment is issued after you complete your first week in the program.

What kind of time commitment is required?

We recommend dedicating 5-10 hours per week to create and manage your advertising campaigns. The program is designed to be flexible around your existing schedule.

Do I need a large following to participate?

No! We work with creators of all audience sizes. What matters most is your engagement rate and willingness to learn effective advertising strategies.
